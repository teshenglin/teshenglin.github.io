Research Interests

    Modeling
    • Instabilities in thin fluid films.
    • Thin films of nematic liquid crystals.

    Scientific computation
    • Numerical methods for nonlinear PDEs: nonlinear Schrodinger equation, high order nonlinear diffusion equation.
    • Computational fluid dynamics: Immersed boundary method, Immersed interface method.
    • Curve construction: Non-Uniform-Rational B-spline (NURBs).
