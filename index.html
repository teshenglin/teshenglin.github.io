About me

I am an associate professor at the Department of Applied Mathematics, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, Taiwan. My research focuses on developing analytical, computational, and machine-learning methodologies for challenging scientific computing tasks, and further communicating with scientists from other disciplines to address practical problems.

My list of publications in Google Scholar. Some preprints in arXiv.

I co-organize NYCU webinar on Scientific Machine Learning.

Interests

  • Scientific computation
